Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!
