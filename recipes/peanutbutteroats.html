Peanut Butter and Yogurt Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 banana sliced
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons strawberry jam
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine oats, banana, cinnamon, and salt in a microwave-safe bowl
  2. Add water and stir
  3. Cook in microwave for 2-3 minutes until water is absorbed
  4. Stir to evenly distribute cinnamon
  5. Let stand for roughly 3 minutes until slightly cooled
  6. Spoon Greek yogurt, strawberry jam, and peanut butter over oatmeal
  7. Stir and enjoy
