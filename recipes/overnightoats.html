Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons chia seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup fresh bluebrries

Steps

  1. Combine milk, yogurt, oats, honey, chia seeds, and cinnamon in a 1/2-pint jar with a lid
  2. Cover and shake until combined
  3. Fold in blueberries
  4. Cover and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight. Enjoy!
